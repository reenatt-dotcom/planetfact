Overview

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and is known for its prominent bands and the Great Red Spot.

Internal Structure

Jupiter has a dense core, surrounded by metallic hydrogen, and an outer layer of molecular hydrogen.

Surface Geology

Jupiter has a turbulent atmosphere with various cloud bands and storms, including the Great Red Spot.
