Overview

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance.

Internal Structure

Mars has a core, a mantle, and a crust, similar to Earth, although its core is not as active.

Surface Geology

Mars has a diverse surface with volcanoes, canyons, polar ice caps, and dust storms.
