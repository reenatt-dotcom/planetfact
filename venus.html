Overview

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is known for its thick atmosphere and extreme temperatures.

Internal Structure

Venus has a rocky mantle and a metallic core, similar to Earth.

Surface Geology

Venus has a relatively young surface with volcanic features, such as volcanoes, lava flows, and impact craters.
