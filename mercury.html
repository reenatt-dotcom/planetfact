Overview

Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun. It is a rocky planet with a cratered surface.

Internal Structure

Mercury has a solid, iron-rich core and a thin rocky mantle.

Surface Geology

Mercury's surface is heavily cratered and has extensive plains and cliffs.
